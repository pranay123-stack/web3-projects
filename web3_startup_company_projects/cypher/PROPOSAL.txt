                          THE PROBLEM
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Every successful DeFi protocol needs transparency. Without it:

    ✗ LPs can't track their positions or calculate impermanent loss
    ✗ Traders don't know which pools have deepest liquidity
    ✗ The team can't showcase growth metrics to investors
    ✗ Users leave for protocols with better tooling
    ✗ Community trust erodes over time

Right now, Cypher users must rely on:

    → Generic tools like DefiLlama (no Cypher-specific features)
    → Dune Analytics (requires SQL knowledge)
    → Manual blockchain exploration (time-consuming)
