## Detailed Bot Categories

### 1. MEV Bots (Maximal Extractable Value)

Bots that extract value by manipulating transaction ordering in the mempool.

**Examples:** Flashbots searchers, Jito bundles (Solana)

**Types:**
| Type | Strategy | Risk Level |
|------|----------|------------|
| **Frontrunning** | Buy before victim's large buy | High |
| **Sandwich** | Buy before + sell after victim | High |
| **Backrunning** | Execute right after profitable event | Medium |
| **JIT Liquidity** | Provide liquidity just-in-time | Medium |
